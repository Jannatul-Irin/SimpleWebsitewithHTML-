NUHAS POLLI

Nuhash Polli is a farm house which was build by Humayun Ahmed. Who is the most famous writer, novelist, film maker of Bangladesh. It was his dream project. He use this place as shooting spot of his films and TV dramas. It was his second place. He loved this place as his children. So that he wanted to live there after his death. As per his last wish he buried here. After his death this place became more popular. Many people go there only show respect to his graveyard. Nuhash palli is good for day out also. There is a lot of trees. A big water body. And there is night stay facility also.
